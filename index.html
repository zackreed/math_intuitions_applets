Math Intuitions Applets

Interactive tools for learning mathematical concepts

Available Walkthroughs

  • Singular Value Decomposition (SVD)

    Explore SVD through vector projections and interactive visualizations. Includes SageMath computations and a quiz.

  • Interactive Derivatives Explorer

    Visualize the relationship between functions and their derivatives. Explore tangent lines, rates of change, and symbolic differentiation.

  • Tortoise vs Hare: Rates of Change

    Race a Tortoise against a Hare to understand average speed. Design your own race strategy!

  • Geometric Series: Convergence

    Explore how infinite sums can have finite values through rectangle subdivisions. Learn about monotonic bounded sequences and convergence.

  • Chain Rule: Composition and Rate Propagation

    Visualize how rates of change multiply through composed functions. Interactive dual-canvas demonstration with zoomed differentials.

  • Geometric Series: Circle Sectors

    Visualize geometric series convergence through circle sector subdivision. Watch partial sums approach their limit!

  • Local Linearity: Limits of Average Rates

    Zoom in on functions to see them become linear. Understand derivatives as limits of difference quotients.

  • Population Density: Modeling with Integrals

    Explore how integrals accumulate density functions to find total population. Interactive city visualization with Riemann sums.

About

This repository contains a collection of interactive applets designed to build intuition for various mathematical concepts. Each walkthrough combines interactive visualizations, computational tools, and quizzes to reinforce learning.
